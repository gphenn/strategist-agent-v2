# Strategic Discovery: Baie Verte Economic Development Project

## Project Readiness Assessment - Dimension 4: Outcome Expectations

**Date:** January 2025  
**Strategic Context:** Regional Economic Development + Mining Services  
**Active Modules:** Regional Economic Development Strategic Module, Mining Services Economic Development Module

---

## Executive Summary

The Baie Verte Economic Development Project has clearly defined outcome expectations focused on maximizing local economic benefits from Firefly Metals' mine expansion. The project aims to capture 80-85% of mining service contracts locally, achieve a 1.5-2x employment multiplier, and secure significant infrastructure investment while building sustainable local capacity.

**Outcome Complexity:** High - Multiple success metrics across service capture, employment, infrastructure, business development, and workforce capacity building.

---

## 1. Primary Success Metrics

### Service Capture Target

**Primary Goal:** 80-85% of mining service contracts captured locally
**Measurement:** Percentage of total service contracts awarded to local businesses
**Strategic Importance:** Core economic development objective and primary success indicator

### Employment Multiplier

**Target:** 1 Firefly employee = 1.5-2 local jobs
**Refinement Required:** Adjust multiplier based on service business mapping and opportunities
**Methodology:** Estimate service business capacity before finalizing multiplier
**Strategic Context:** Key indicator of local economic impact and capacity utilization

### Infrastructure Development

**Critical Infrastructure Targets:**

1. **Baie Verte Highway** - Safety and reliability upgrades
2. **Cell Service Coverage** - Complete coverage along highway
3. **Healthcare Coverage** - Hospital capacity expansion for mine operations
4. **Energy/Power Increase** - Transmission line upgrades for town + mine expansion
5. **Town Water & Sewer** - Capacity assessment and upgrade planning
6. **Town Road Upgrades** - Gradual improvement of municipal roads
7. **Municipal Services** - Recreation and quality-of-life services

**Success Criteria:** All critical infrastructure upgrades completed and operational

## 10. Success Measurement Framework

### Key Performance Indicators

1. **Service Capture Rate:** Percentage of contracts awarded to local businesses (Target: 80-85%)
2. **Employment Multiplier:** Actual local jobs created per Firefly employee (Target: 1.5-2x)
3. **Infrastructure Investment:** Dollars invested in critical infrastructure
4. **Business Creation:** Number of new businesses established
5. **Revenue Generation:** Total revenue generated by local service businesses
6. **Workforce Development:** Number of workers trained and retained
7. **Funding Secured:** Total funding obtained from all sources
8. **Timeline Adherence:** Services operational according to Firefly requirements

### Monitoring Framework

- **Monthly:** Service contract awards and employment numbers
- **Quarterly:** Infrastructure progress and business development
- **Annually:** Comprehensive economic impact assessment
- **Ongoing:** Stakeholder feedback and adjustment

### Success Validation

- **Quantitative Metrics:** All KPIs meeting or exceeding targets
- **Qualitative Assessment:** Stakeholder satisfaction and relationship strength
- **Sustainability Indicators:** Long-term economic resilience and diversification
- **Regional Impact:** Positive broader regional development outcomes
