## Document Purpose

This document provides a framework for monitoring risks throughout the project lifecycle. The framework will be implemented and refined based on monitoring experience.

**Objective:** Establish comprehensive risk monitoring to enable early identification of risk changes, effective mitigation, and timely response.

---

## Monitoring Framework Overview

### Monitoring Objectives

**Primary Objectives:**
- Early identification of risk changes
- Effective mitigation strategy monitoring
- Timely risk response
- Continuous risk assessment improvement

**Monitoring Scope:**
- All identified risks
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Early warning indicators
- Risk trends and patterns

---

## 1. Risk Monitoring Structure

### 1.1 Monitoring Levels

**Level 1: Continuous Monitoring**
- **Frequency:** Ongoing
- **Scope:** Critical risks, high-priority risks
- **Method:** Automated monitoring, regular checks
- **Responsibility:** Risk owners, project team

**Level 2: Regular Monitoring**
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Scope:** All high and medium priority risks
- **Method:** Weekly risk reviews, status updates
- **Responsibility:** Risk owners, project manager

**Level 3: Periodic Monitoring**
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Scope:** All risks
- **Method:** Monthly risk reviews, risk register updates
- **Responsibility:** Risk management working group

**Level 4: Comprehensive Monitoring**
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Scope:** Complete risk portfolio
- **Method:** Comprehensive risk assessment, trend analysis
- **Responsibility:** Steering committee, executive sponsor

---

### 1.2 Monitoring Responsibilities

**Risk Owners:**
- Monitor assigned risks continuously
- Update risk status regularly
- Report risk changes immediately
- Implement mitigation strategies

**Project Manager:**
- Coordinate risk monitoring
- Review risk status weekly
- Escalate critical risks
- Report to steering committee

**Risk Management Working Group:**
- Conduct monthly risk reviews
- Update risk register
- Assess mitigation effectiveness
- Identify new risks

**Steering Committee:**
- Review risk portfolio quarterly
- Approve risk management decisions
- Provide risk management guidance
- Escalate to executive sponsor

---

## 2. Risk Monitoring Methods

### 2.1 Risk Status Monitoring

**Status Indicators:**
- **Open:** Risk active, mitigation in progress
- **Mitigated:** Risk mitigated, monitoring ongoing
- **Closed:** Risk resolved, no longer active
- **Escalated:** Risk escalated for attention

**Monitoring Process:**
1. Assess risk status
2. Update risk register
3. Report status changes
4. Take action as needed

**Frequency:** Weekly for high-priority risks, monthly for all risks

---

### 2.2 Early Warning Indicator Monitoring

**Early Warning Indicators:**
- Risk probability changes
- Risk impact changes
- Mitigation strategy effectiveness
- Trigger point proximity

**Monitoring Process:**
1. Monitor early warning indicators
2. Assess indicator significance
3. Report indicator changes
4. Take preventive action

**Frequency:** Continuous for critical risks, weekly for high-priority risks

---

### 2.3 Mitigation Strategy Monitoring

**Mitigation Monitoring:**
- Mitigation action completion
- Mitigation effectiveness
- Resource utilization
- Timeline adherence

**Monitoring Process:**
1. Track mitigation actions
2. Assess mitigation effectiveness
3. Report mitigation status
4. Adjust mitigation as needed

**Frequency:** Weekly for high-priority mitigations, monthly for all mitigations

---

### 2.4 Risk Trend Monitoring

**Trend Analysis:**
- Risk score changes
- New risk identification
- Risk closure rate
- Risk category distribution

**Monitoring Process:**
1. Analyze risk trends
2. Identify patterns
3. Report trends
4. Take strategic action

**Frequency:** Monthly trend analysis, quarterly comprehensive analysis

---

## 3. Risk Monitoring Procedures

### 3.1 Weekly Risk Monitoring

**Weekly Risk Review Process:**
1. **Review Critical and High Risks:**
   - Assess risk status
   - Review mitigation progress
   - Identify changes
   - Update risk register

2. **Check Early Warning Indicators:**
   - Monitor indicators
   - Assess significance
   - Report changes
   - Take action

3. **Update Risk Status:**
   - Update risk register
   - Report status changes
   - Communicate updates
   - Document changes

4. **Weekly Risk Report:**
   - Prepare weekly report
   - Distribute to project team
   - Highlight critical items
   - Track action items

**Responsibility:** Project Manager  
**Timeline:** Weekly  
**Output:** Weekly Risk Report

---

### 3.2 Monthly Risk Monitoring

**Monthly Risk Review Process:**
1. **Comprehensive Risk Review:**
   - Review all risks
   - Assess risk status
   - Update risk scores
   - Identify new risks

2. **Mitigation Effectiveness Assessment:**
   - Assess mitigation effectiveness
   - Identify improvement opportunities
   - Adjust mitigation strategies
   - Update mitigation plans

3. **Risk Trend Analysis:**
   - Analyze risk trends
   - Identify patterns
   - Assess portfolio health
   - Report trends

4. **Monthly Risk Dashboard:**
   - Prepare monthly dashboard
   - Distribute to steering committee
   - Highlight key findings
   - Track progress

**Responsibility:** Risk Management Working Group  
**Timeline:** Monthly  
**Output:** Monthly Risk Dashboard

---

### 3.3 Quarterly Risk Monitoring

**Quarterly Risk Assessment Process:**
1. **Comprehensive Risk Assessment:**
   - Complete risk portfolio review
   - Assess overall risk exposure
   - Evaluate risk management effectiveness
   - Identify strategic risks

2. **Risk Management Review:**
   - Review risk management process
   - Assess process effectiveness
   - Identify improvements
   - Update process

3. **Strategic Risk Alignment:**
   - Assess strategic risk alignment
   - Review risk tolerance
   - Adjust risk management approach
   - Report to executive sponsor

4. **Quarterly Risk Report:**
   - Prepare quarterly report
   - Distribute to executive leadership
   - Highlight strategic findings
   - Track strategic actions

**Responsibility:** Steering Committee  
**Timeline:** Quarterly  
**Output:** Quarterly Risk Report

## 5. Risk Escalation

### 5.1 Escalation Criteria

**Immediate Escalation:**
- New critical risk identified
- Existing risk increases to critical level
- Risk event occurs
- Multiple risks in same category reach high level

**Standard Escalation:**
- Risk status changes significantly
- Mitigation strategy ineffective
- Resource constraints identified
- Timeline concerns

---

### 5.2 Escalation Procedures

**Escalation Levels:**
- **Level 1:** Project Manager
- **Level 2:** Steering Committee
- **Level 3:** Executive Sponsor
- **Level 4:** Municipal Council / Firefly Metals Leadership

**Escalation Process:**
1. Identify escalation need
2. Assess escalation level
3. Prepare escalation communication
4. Escalate to appropriate level
5. Track escalation resolution
