## Document Purpose

This document provides a funding strategy framework for economic development initiatives. The strategy will be refined with detailed funding program research and identification.

**Objective:** Develop comprehensive funding strategy to secure funding for infrastructure, service expansion, business development, and workforce development initiatives.

**Target:** Secure funding for significant investment requirements across all project components

- **Timeline:** Immediate to 6 months

**Priority 2: High Priority Funding (Short-term)**
- Workforce development funding
- Housing development funding
- **Timeline:** 6-12 months

**Priority 3: Medium Priority Funding (Medium-term)**
- Community services funding
- Quality of life funding
- **Timeline:** 12-24 months
