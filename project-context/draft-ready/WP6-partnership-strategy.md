## Document Purpose

This document provides a partnership strategy for workforce development to support local workforce capacity building. The strategy will be refined with partner identification and partnership development.

**Objective:** Establish strategic partnerships to support workforce development, training program delivery, and workforce capacity building.

**Target:** Support 600-800 local job creation and service business development
