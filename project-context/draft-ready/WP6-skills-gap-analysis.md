## Document Purpose

This document provides a framework for analyzing skills gaps between current workforce skills and projected workforce needs. The analysis will be completed after current skills inventory assessment and detailed workforce requirements are available.

**Objective:** Identify skills gaps to guide training program development and workforce development strategies.

**Target:** Support 600-800 local job creation (1.5-2x employment multiplier) and service business development

---

## Skills Gap Analysis Methodology

### Analysis Framework

**Step 1: Current Skills Inventory**
- Assess current skills available in workforce
- Document skills distribution
- Identify skills strengths

**Step 2: Required Skills Assessment**
- Determine skills required for projected jobs
- Document skills requirements by job category
- Identify skills priorities

**Step 3: Gap Calculation**
- Compare current skills vs. required skills
- Calculate skills gaps
- Quantify gap severity

**Step 4: Gap Prioritization**
- Prioritize gaps by urgency
- Prioritize gaps by impact
- Prioritize gaps by training feasibility

## Skills Gap Prioritization

### Priority 1: Critical Gaps (Spring-Summer 2026)

**Skills Required:**
- Food service certification and skills
- Transportation (commercial driver's license)
- Laundry services skills
- Cleaning services skills
- Customer service skills

**Rationale:** Required for Spring-Summer 2026 service business development

**Training Timeline:** Immediate to Spring-Summer 2026

### Priority 2: High Priority Gaps

**Skills Required:**

- Maintenance and repair
