This document provides a framework for assessing municipal investment capacity to support economic development initiatives. The assessment will be completed after financial position analysis.

**Objective:** Determine municipal capacity to invest in infrastructure, service expansion, and economic development initiatives.

**Target:** Support significant investment requirements for infrastructure and service expansion
