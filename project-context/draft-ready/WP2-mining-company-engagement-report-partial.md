## 10. Key Findings & Recommendations

### 10.1 Key Findings

1. **Expansion Scale**: Firefly Metals is planning a significant expansion, tripling workforce from 100 to 400+ FIFO workers over 5 years.

2. **Timeline Urgency**: Camp construction services are needed by Spring-Summer 2026, creating urgent timeline for service provider readiness.

3. **Service Opportunities**: Multiple service opportunities identified:
   - Camp cafeteria services
   - Transportation services
   - Laundry services
   - Cleaning services
   - Other mining support services

4. **Local Content Commitment**: Firefly has demonstrated commitment to local hiring and community engagement, supporting 80-85% local service capture target.

5. **Workforce Challenges**: Trade worker and service worker shortages in Newfoundland present challenges that need to be addressed.

6. **Infrastructure Needs**: Significant infrastructure requirements identified: highway, power, healthcare, water/sewer, roads.

### 10.2 Recommendations

1. **Immediate Action**: Schedule direct engagement meeting with Firefly Metals to:
   - Confirm detailed service requirements and specifications
   - Understand procurement process and timelines
   - Clarify contract terms and evaluation criteria
   - Validate workforce projections and requirements

2. **Service Provider Readiness**: Begin capacity building for local service providers to meet Spring-Summer 2026 timeline.

3. **Workforce Development**: Address workforce shortages through training programs and recruitment strategies.

4. **Infrastructure Planning**: Coordinate infrastructure development with Firefly's expansion timeline.

5. **Partnership Development**: Explore partnership opportunities with Firefly Metals for service delivery.
