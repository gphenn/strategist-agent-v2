## Document Purpose

This document provides training program recommendations to address skills gaps and build local workforce capacity. Recommendations will be refined with detailed training needs assessment and skills gap analysis.

**Objective:** Provide actionable training program recommendations to support workforce development and service business readiness.

**Target:** Support 600-800 local job creation and Spring-Summer 2026 service business development

- Ongoing availability
- Continuous improvement
