## Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Categories

- **Timeline & Schedule Risks:** Project timeline, deadlines, dependencies
- **Resource & Workforce Risks:** Workforce availability, skills, competition
- **Infrastructure Risks:** Infrastructure capacity, funding, completion
- **Regulatory & Compliance Risks:** Consultation, compliance, certification
- **Financial & Funding Risks:** Funding availability, financial capacity
- **Operational Risks:** Service business capacity, quality, housing, services
- **Market & Competition Risks:** Market conditions, competition, mining project
- **Social & Community Risks:** Community relations, regional coordination

### Risk Scoring Matrix

**Probability Scale (1-5):**
- **1 - Very Low:** 0-10% chance of occurring
- **2 - Low:** 10-25% chance of occurring
- **3 - Medium:** 25-50% chance of occurring
- **4 - High:** 50-75% chance of occurring
- **5 - Very High:** 75-100% chance of occurring

**Impact Scale (1-5):**
- **1 - Negligible:** Minimal impact on objectives
- **2 - Minor:** Small impact, easily managed
- **3 - Moderate:** Noticeable impact requiring management attention
- **4 - Major:** Significant impact affecting key objectives
- **5 - Severe:** Critical impact threatening project success

**Risk Level Calculation:** Risk Score = Probability Ã— Impact (1-25 scale)

**Risk Priority Levels:**
- **Critical (20-25):** Immediate action required
- **High (15-19):** Urgent attention needed
- **Medium (8-14):** Monitor and manage actively
- **Low (4-7):** Monitor periodically
- **Very Low (1-3):** Minimal monitoring required

## Detailed Risk Analysis

### Risk ID: R001

**Risk Title:** Services Not Ready by Spring-Summer 2026 Deadline

**Risk Description:** Services must be operational when Firefly needs them (Spring-Summer 2026 start for camp construction). Delays in service business development, infrastructure completion, or workforce readiness could result in missed opportunities and lost service contracts.

**Risk Category:** Timeline & Schedule

**Risk Triggers:**
- Infrastructure completion delays
- Workforce development delays
- Service business development delays
- Funding application delays

**Risk Assessment:**
- **Probability:** 4 (High) - Aggressive timeline with multiple dependencies
- **Impact:** 5 (Severe) - Could result in lost service contracts and economic opportunities
- **Risk Score:** 20
- **Priority Level:** Critical

**Impact Analysis:**
- **Financial Impact:** Potential loss of service contracts, reduced economic multiplier
- **Schedule Impact:** Could delay entire project timeline
- **Quality Impact:** Rushed implementation could affect service quality
- **Stakeholder Impact:** Firefly Metals, local businesses, community
- **Strategic Impact:** Could prevent achieving 80-85% service capture target

**Root Causes:**
- Aggressive timeline with multiple dependencies
- Infrastructure upgrades required
- Workforce development needed
- Service business capacity building required

**Risk Response Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Actions:**
1. **Develop detailed timeline with buffer time**
   - Description: Create detailed project timeline with buffer time for critical activities
   - Owner: Project Manager
   - Due Date: February 2025
   - Status: Not Started
   - Effectiveness: Reduce probability to Medium (3)

2. **Implement parallel activities where possible**
   - Description: Identify activities that can proceed in parallel to reduce timeline
   - Owner: Project Manager
   - Due Date: February 2025
   - Status: Not Started
   - Effectiveness: Reduce timeline pressure

3. **Prioritize critical path activities**
   - Description: Identify and prioritize activities on critical path
   - Owner: Project Manager
   - Due Date: February 2025
   - Status: Not Started
   - Effectiveness: Focus resources on critical activities

4. **Early start on high-priority items**
   - Description: Start high-priority activities early to build buffer
   - Owner: Project Manager
   - Due Date: Immediate
   - Status: In Progress
   - Effectiveness: Build timeline buffer

5. **Regular progress monitoring**
   - Description: Establish regular progress monitoring and reporting
   - Owner: Project Manager
   - Due Date: February 2025
   - Status: Not Started
   - Effectiveness: Early identification of delays

**Contingency Plan:**
- **Immediate Response:** Identify alternative service providers, extend timeline if possible
- **Recovery Actions:** Accelerate activities, reallocate resources, negotiate timeline extension
- **Resources Required:** Additional resources, funding, coordination
- **Communication Plan:** Notify Firefly Metals, stakeholders, adjust expectations

**Early Warning Indicators:**
- Infrastructure completion behind schedule
- Workforce development behind schedule
- Service business development behind schedule
- Funding applications delayed

**Monitoring Plan:**
- **Review Frequency:** Weekly

## Risk Governance

### Risk Review Process

**Monthly Risk Review:**
- Review all critical and high risks
- Update risk scores and mitigation progress
- Identify new risks and early warnings
- Escalate issues requiring senior attention

**Quarterly Risk Assessment:**
- Comprehensive review of entire risk register
- Risk trend analysis and portfolio optimization
- Strategic risk alignment review
- Risk management process improvements

### Escalation Procedures

**Immediate Escalation Triggers:**
- New critical risk identified
- Existing risk increases to critical level
- Multiple risks in same category reach high level
- Risk event occurs requiring crisis response

**Escalation Contacts:**
- **Level 1:** Project Manager
- **Level 2:** Steering Committee
- **Level 3:** Executive Sponsor
- **Level 4:** Municipal Council / Firefly Metals Leadership

### Risk Reporting

**Weekly:** Risk highlight report to project team  
**Monthly:** Risk dashboard to steering committee  
**Quarterly:** Risk portfolio report to executive leadership  
**Ad-hoc:** Critical risk alerts and incident reports
