## Executive Summary

### Assessment Overview

**Assessment Purpose:** Evaluate current workforce capacity and develop strategies to build local workforce capacity to support Firefly Metals mining expansion and associated economic development, including service business development.

**Assessment Scope:** Comprehensive assessment of current labor market, skills inventory, projected workforce needs, skills gaps, and workforce development strategies.

**Key Findings:**
- Current labor force: Limited local workforce, low unemployment rate (17.9% in 2016)
- Workforce constraints: Trade worker and service worker shortages in Newfoundland
- Projected job growth: 600-800 local jobs (1.5-2x employment multiplier)
- Skills gaps: [To be assessed - requires skills inventory]
- Training needs: [To be assessed - requires training needs assessment]

**Critical Skills Gaps:**
- Current skills inventory assessment required
- Training needs assessment required
- Educational infrastructure assessment required

**Priority Recommendations:**
1. Complete current skills inventory assessment

4. Establish partnership strategy with educational institutions
