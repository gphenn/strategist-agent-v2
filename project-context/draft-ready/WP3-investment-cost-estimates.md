## Document Purpose

This document provides cost estimates for infrastructure development requirements. Cost estimates will be developed after infrastructure development requirements are defined and detailed technical specifications are available.

**Objective:** Provide comprehensive cost estimates to support infrastructure investment planning, funding applications, and budget development.

**Note:** All cost estimates are preliminary and require detailed engineering assessments and quotes from contractors and suppliers.

---

## Cost Estimation Methodology

### Estimation Approach

**Level 1 - Order of Magnitude Estimates:**
- Based on similar projects
- High-level assumptions
- Accuracy: ±50%
- **Use:** Initial planning, feasibility assessment

**Level 2 - Budget Estimates:**
- Based on preliminary design
- More detailed assumptions
- Accuracy: ±25%
- **Use:** Budget planning, funding applications

**Level 3 - Detailed Estimates:**
- Based on detailed design
- Specific quotes and bids
- Accuracy: ±10%
- **Use:** Final budget, contract negotiation

**Current Status:** Level 1 - Order of Magnitude (Framework)
