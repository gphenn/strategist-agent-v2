## Document Purpose

This document provides investment recommendations for community services expansion. Recommendations will be developed after expansion requirements are defined and detailed cost estimates are available.

**Objective:** Provide comprehensive investment recommendations to support service expansion planning and funding applications.

**Target:** Support population growth from 1,315 to 2,311-2,511 (projected) and workforce expansion
