## Document Purpose

This document provides a framework for assessing capacity gaps in community services. The assessment will be completed after current capacity assessment and projected demand analysis.

**Objective:** Identify capacity gaps to guide service expansion planning and investment decisions.

**Target:** Support population growth from 1,315 to 2,311-2,511 (projected) and workforce expansion

---

## Capacity Gap Analysis Methodology

### Analysis Framework

**Step 1: Current Capacity Assessment**
- Assess current service capacity
- Document capacity metrics
- Calculate utilization rates
- Identify reserve capacity

**Step 2: Required Capacity Assessment**
- Project service demand
- Calculate required capacity
- Account for growth projections
- Consider service standards

**Step 3: Gap Calculation**
- Compare current capacity vs. required capacity
- Calculate capacity gaps
- Assess gap severity
- Identify critical gaps

**Step 4: Gap Prioritization**
- Prioritize gaps by urgency
- Prioritize gaps by impact
- Prioritize gaps by strategic importance
