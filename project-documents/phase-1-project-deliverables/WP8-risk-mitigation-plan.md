## Document Purpose

This document provides a framework for identifying and mitigating financial risks associated with economic development initiatives. The plan will be refined with detailed risk assessment.

**Objective:** Identify financial risks and develop mitigation strategies to protect municipal financial position and ensure project success.

**Target:** Minimize financial risks and ensure sustainable financial management

---

## Risk Mitigation Framework

### Risk Management Process

**Risk Management Steps:**
1. **Risk Identification:** Identify all financial risks
2. **Risk Assessment:** Assess probability and impact
3. **Risk Mitigation:** Develop mitigation strategies
4. **Risk Monitoring:** Monitor risks ongoing
5. **Risk Response:** Respond to risks as they occur
