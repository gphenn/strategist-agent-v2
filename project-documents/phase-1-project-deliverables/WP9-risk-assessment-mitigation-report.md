## Executive Summary

### Assessment Overview

**Assessment Purpose:** Identify, assess, and develop mitigation strategies for risks associated with the Baie Verte economic development project to support Firefly Metals mining expansion.

**Assessment Scope:** Comprehensive risk assessment across all project dimensions including timeline, resources, infrastructure, regulatory, workforce, financial, and operational risks.

**Key Findings:**
- **Total Risks Identified:** 25+ risks across 8 risk categories
- **High-Risk Items:** Timeline pressure, workforce shortages, infrastructure constraints, regulatory consultation
- **Medium-Risk Items:** Funding availability, competition, service capacity
- **Low-Risk Items:** Various operational and market risks
- **Overall Risk Rating:** Medium-High (due to timeline pressure and resource constraints)

**Critical Risks Identified:**
1. **Timeline Risk:** Services must be operational by Spring-Summer 2026
2. **Workforce Risk:** Limited local workforce, trade/service worker shortages
3. **Infrastructure Risk:** Current infrastructure insufficient for expansion
4. **Regulatory Risk:** Qalipu First Nation consultation required
5. **Funding Risk:** Significant funding requirements across multiple areas

**Priority Mitigation Actions:**
1. Develop timeline buffer and parallel activity plans
2. Implement workforce development and recruitment strategies
3. Prioritize critical infrastructure investments
4. Initiate early regulatory consultation
5. Diversify funding sources and applications

## Risk Categories & Framework

### Risk Assessment Framework

#### Risk Probability Scale

- **Very Low (1):** <10% chance of occurrence
- **Low (2):** 10-25% chance of occurrence
- **Medium (3):** 25-50% chance of occurrence
- **High (4):** 50-75% chance of occurrence
- **Very High (5):** >75% chance of occurrence

#### Risk Impact Scale

- **Very Low (1):** Minimal impact on project objectives
- **Low (2):** Minor impact, easily manageable
- **Medium (3):** Moderate impact, requires attention
- **High (4):** Significant impact, major disruption
- **Very High (5):** Critical impact, project failure

#### Risk Rating Matrix

| Impact | Very Low (1) | Low (2) | Medium (3) | High (4) | Very High (5) |
|--------|--------------|---------|------------|----------|---------------|
| **Very High (5)** | Medium | High | High | Critical | Critical |
| **High (4)** | Low | Medium | High | High | Critical |
| **Medium (3)** | Low | Low | Medium | High | High |
| **Low (2)** | Low | Low | Low | Medium | High |
| **Very Low (1)** | Low | Low | Low | Low | Medium |

**Risk Rating Definitions:**
- **Critical:** Immediate action required, high probability and very high impact
- **High:** Significant action required, high probability or high impact
- **Medium:** Moderate action required, medium probability and/or impact
- **Low:** Monitor, low probability and/or impact

## 1. Timeline & Schedule Risks

### 1.1 Critical Timeline Risks

#### Risk 1.1: Services Not Ready by Spring-Summer 2026 Deadline

**Risk Description:** Services must be operational when Firefly needs them (Spring-Summer 2026 start for camp construction). Delays in service business development, infrastructure completion, or workforce readiness could result in missed opportunities.

**Probability:** High (4) - Timeline is aggressive with multiple dependencies

**Impact:** Very High (5) - Could result in lost service contracts and economic opportunities

**Risk Rating:** Critical

**Timeframe:** Immediate to Short-term (2025-2026)

**Affected Areas:**
- Service business development
- Infrastructure completion
- Workforce readiness
- Camp construction support

**Root Causes:**
- Aggressive timeline with multiple dependencies
- Infrastructure upgrades required
- Workforce development needed
- Service business capacity building required

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Develop detailed timeline with buffer time
- Implement parallel activities where possible
- Prioritize critical path activities
- Early start on high-priority items
- Regular progress monitoring

**Status:** ⚠️ Requires detailed timeline development and monitoring

---

#### Risk 1.2: Infrastructure Completion Delays

**Risk Description:** Infrastructure upgrades (highway, power, healthcare, water/sewer, roads) may not be completed in time to support service operations and population growth.

**Probability:** Medium (3) - Infrastructure projects subject to delays

**Impact:** High (4) - Could constrain service delivery and population growth

**Risk Rating:** High

**Timeframe:** Short-term to Medium-term (2025-2027)

**Affected Areas:**
- Infrastructure capacity
- Service delivery
- Population growth support
- Economic development

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Prioritize critical infrastructure
- Secure funding early
- Coordinate with infrastructure providers
- Develop contingency plans
- Monitor progress closely

**Status:** ⚠️ Requires infrastructure timeline coordination

---

### 1.2 Schedule Dependency Risks
