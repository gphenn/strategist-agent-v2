## Risk Assessment Framework

### Risk Categories

- **Strategic Risks:** Market changes, competitive threats, business model risks
- **Operational Risks:** Process failures, resource constraints, execution risks
- **Financial Risks:** Budget overruns, revenue shortfalls, cash flow issues
- **Technology Risks:** System failures, cybersecurity, technical feasibility
- **Regulatory Risks:** Compliance failures, regulatory changes, legal issues
- **Reputational Risks:** Brand damage, stakeholder confidence, public relations
- **Human Resources Risks:** Key person dependency, talent retention, skill gaps
- **External Risks:** Economic conditions, natural disasters, supplier issues
- **Infrastructure Risks:** Infrastructure capacity, development delays, cost overruns
- **Stakeholder Risks:** Stakeholder opposition, relationship deterioration, communication failures

### Risk Scoring Matrix

**Probability Scale (1-5):**

- **1 - Very Low:** 0-5% chance of occurring
- **2 - Low:** 6-25% chance of occurring  
- **3 - Medium:** 26-50% chance of occurring
- **4 - High:** 51-75% chance of occurring
- **5 - Very High:** 76-100% chance of occurring

**Impact Scale (1-5):**

- **1 - Negligible:** Minimal impact on objectives
- **2 - Minor:** Small impact, easily managed
- **3 - Moderate:** Noticeable impact requiring management attention
- **4 - Major:** Significant impact affecting key objectives
- **5 - Severe:** Critical impact threatening project/business success

**Risk Level Calculation:** Risk Score = Probability Ã— Impact (1-25 scale)

**Risk Priority Levels:**

- **Critical (20-25):** Immediate action required
- **High (15-19):** Urgent attention needed
- **Medium (8-14):** Monitor and manage actively
- **Low (4-7):** Monitor periodically
- **Very Low (1-3):** Minimal monitoring required

## Detailed Risk Analysis

### Risk ID: R001

**Risk Title:** Services Not Operational by Spring-Summer 2026 Deadline

**Risk Description:** Camp construction services must be operational when Firefly Metals needs them (Spring-Summer 2026 start). Failure to meet this deadline could result in loss of service contract opportunities, damage to relationship with Firefly Metals, and failure to achieve 80-85% local service capture target.

**Risk Category:** Operational

**Risk Triggers:**
- Delays in business capacity building
- Infrastructure development delays
- Workforce development delays
- Funding delays
- Certification and quality standard delays

**Risk Assessment:**
- **Probability:** 4 (High) - Timeline is tight, multiple dependencies
- **Impact:** 5 (Severe) - Critical impact on project success
- **Risk Score:** 20
- **Priority Level:** Critical

**Impact Analysis:**
- **Financial Impact:** Loss of service contract revenue, potential project failure
- **Schedule Impact:** Project delays, missed opportunities
- **Quality Impact:** Reduced service quality due to rushed implementation
- **Stakeholder Impact:** Damage to relationship with Firefly Metals, loss of community support
- **Strategic Impact:** Failure to achieve 80-85% local service capture target

**Root Causes:**
- Tight timeline (Spring-Summer 2026)
- Multiple dependencies (infrastructure, workforce, business capacity)
- Limited existing capacity
- Resource constraints

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- **Prevention:**
  - Accelerate business capacity building programs
  - Prioritize critical infrastructure development
  - Early workforce development initiatives
  - Proactive funding strategy
  - Parallel work streams where possible
- **Response:**
  - Contingency planning for alternative service providers (temporary)
  - Accelerated implementation plans
  - Additional resource allocation
  - Stakeholder communication and expectation management

**Contingency Plans:**
- Temporary service provider arrangements if local capacity not ready
- Phased service implementation
- Extended timeline negotiation with Firefly Metals (if possible)

**Monitoring:**
- Weekly timeline tracking
- Monthly milestone reviews
- Early warning indicators for delays

**Owner:** Project Manager  
**Status:** Open  
**Next Review:** February 2025

- High demand for workers across Newfoundland
- Low unemployment rate
- Limited local workforce
- Competition for skilled workers
- Insufficient training programs

**Risk Assessment:**
- **Probability:** 4 (High) - Known constraint identified in strategic context brief
- **Impact:** 4 (Major) - Significant impact on capacity building
- **Risk Score:** 16
- **Priority Level:** High

**Impact Analysis:**

- **Schedule Impact:** Delays in capacity building and service readiness
- **Quality Impact:** Reduced service quality due to insufficient staffing
- **Stakeholder Impact:** Inability to meet Firefly Metals service requirements
- **Strategic Impact:** Failure to achieve local service capture targets

**Root Causes:**
- Trade worker and service worker shortages in Newfoundland
- Limited local workforce
- Low unemployment rate
- Competition for workers
- Insufficient training and development programs

**Mitigation Strategies:**

  - Training and certification programs

  - Retention strategies
  - Partnership with educational institutions
  - Attraction strategies for new workers
- **Response:**

**Contingency Plans:**

**Monitoring:**
