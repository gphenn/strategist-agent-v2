## Document Purpose

This document provides detailed mitigation strategies for identified risks. Strategies will be refined with stakeholder input and technical assessments.

**Objective:** Develop comprehensive mitigation strategies to reduce risk probability and/or impact, ensuring project success.

---

## Mitigation Strategy Framework

### Strategy Categories

**Mitigation Strategy Types:**
- **Avoid:** Eliminate the risk by changing approach
- **Mitigate:** Reduce probability and/or impact
- **Transfer:** Transfer risk to another party
- **Accept:** Accept the risk and monitor

**Strategy Development Process:**
1. Identify mitigation options
2. Assess effectiveness
3. Assess feasibility
4. Select optimal strategies
5. Develop implementation plans
6. Monitor effectiveness
