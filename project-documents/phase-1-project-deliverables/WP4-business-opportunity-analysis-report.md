## Executive Summary

### Opportunity Overview

The Baie Verte Economic Development Project presents significant business opportunities for local businesses to capture 80-85% of Firefly Metals mining service contracts. With Firefly Metals planning to expand from 100 to 400+ FIFO workers over 5 years, substantial service demand will be created for camp cafeteria, transportation, laundry, cleaning, and other mining support services.

**Target Market:** Firefly Metals mining operations and 400+ FIFO workers  
**Value Proposition:** Local service delivery supporting community economic development  
**Key Competitive Advantages:** Local presence, community relationships, alignment with Firefly's local content commitment  
**Expected Market Size:** Service contracts for 400+ workers, 80-85% local capture target

### Key Findings

1. **Current Business Landscape:** 15+ businesses generating $23.4M+ in annual revenue, primarily retail-focused
2. **Service Gaps:** Significant gaps in mining support services (camp cafeteria, transportation, laundry, cleaning)
3. **Expansion Opportunities:** Existing businesses (equipment rental, repair, excavation) can expand into mining services
4. **New Business Opportunities:** Multiple new service businesses needed to meet service demand
5. **Capacity Building Required:** Significant capacity building needed for both new and existing businesses
