## Document Purpose

This framework provides a structured approach to assessing service needs for Firefly Metals' mining expansion. The framework identifies service categories, assessment criteria, and data collection requirements. Detailed service specifications will be populated following direct engagement with Firefly Metals.

**Objective:** Systematically assess and document all service needs to enable local business capacity building and service contract capture.

**Target:** 80-85% of mining service contracts captured locally (from strategic context brief)

---

## Service Categories Overview

### Identified Service Categories

Based on strategic context brief and research data, the following service categories have been identified:

1. **Camp Services**
   - Camp cafeteria/food services
   - Cleaning services
   - Laundry services
   - Maintenance services
   - Security services

2. **Transportation Services**
   - Airport transportation (FIFO workers)
   - Local transportation
   - Equipment transportation
   - Emergency transportation

3. **Support Services**
   - Administrative services
   - Logistics services
   - Supply chain services
   - Waste management services

4. **Professional Services**
   - Engineering services
   - Consulting services
   - Technical services
   - Training services

5. **Construction Services**
   - Camp construction
   - Infrastructure construction
   - Facility construction
   - Site preparation

**Note:** Additional service categories may be identified during direct engagement with Firefly Metals.

## 6. Service Assessment Process

### 6.1 Data Collection

**Step 1: Direct Engagement**
- Schedule meeting with Firefly Metals
- Prepare comprehensive question list
- Conduct engagement meeting
- Document all service requirements

**Step 2: Requirements Documentation**
- Document all service specifications
- Document quality standards
- Document timeline requirements
- Document budget information

**Step 3: Local Business Assessment**
- Assess current local business capacity
- Identify capacity gaps
- Identify capacity building needs
- Assess financial feasibility

### 6.2 Analysis & Prioritization

**Service Prioritization Criteria:**
- **Urgency:** Timeline requirements (Spring-Summer 2026 priority)
- **Volume:** Service volume and value
- **Local Capacity:** Current local business capacity
- **Capacity Building Feasibility:** Ability to build local capacity
- **Strategic Importance:** Alignment with economic development objectives

**Priority Services (Based on Strategic Context Brief):**
1. **High Priority (Spring-Summer 2026):**
   - Camp construction services
   - Camp cafeteria services
   - Transportation services
   - Cleaning services
   - Laundry services

2. **Medium Priority:**
   - Maintenance services
   - Security services
   - Support services

3. **Lower Priority:**
   - Professional services
