## Document Purpose

This document provides financial feasibility assessment templates for each service opportunity. Assessments will be completed with detailed financial data from Firefly Metals engagement, business planning, and market research.

**Objective:** Assess financial feasibility of each service opportunity to support business development decisions and funding applications.

**Target:** 80-85% of mining service contracts captured locally (from strategic context brief)

---

## Financial Feasibility Assessment Framework

### Assessment Components

**For Each Service Opportunity:**

1. **Investment Requirements:**
   - Startup costs
   - Working capital
   - Contingency

2. **Revenue Projections:**
   - Annual revenue
   - Revenue growth
   - Revenue drivers

3. **Operating Costs:**
   - Fixed costs
   - Variable costs
   - Total operating costs

4. **Financial Metrics:**
   - Profitability
   - Return on investment
   - Payback period
   - Break-even analysis

5. **Funding Requirements:**
   - Total investment needed
   - Funding sources
   - Funding strategy
