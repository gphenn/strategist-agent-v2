## Executive Summary

This document analyzes workforce requirements for Firefly Metals' planned mining expansion in Baie Verte, Newfoundland. The analysis is based on available research data and strategic context brief information. A direct engagement meeting with Firefly Metals is required to validate and complete detailed workforce requirements.

**Key Findings:**
- Workforce expansion from ~100 to 400+ FIFO workers over 5 years
- 2-week rotation schedules planned
- Diverse workforce needs including miners, engineers, tradespeople, safety professionals, management, and support staff
- Local hiring commitment supports economic development objectives
- Employment multiplier: 1 Firefly employee = 1.5-2 local jobs (to be refined)
