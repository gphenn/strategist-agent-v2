## Document Purpose

This document provides an implementation plan for workforce development initiatives. The plan will be refined with detailed planning, resource allocation, and timeline development.

**Objective:** Provide actionable implementation plan to guide workforce development execution and ensure readiness for Spring-Summer 2026 service business development.

**Target:** Support 600-800 local job creation and Spring-Summer 2026 service business readiness

---

## Risk Management

### Implementation Risks

**Assessment Risks:**
- **Risk:** Delays in assessment completion
- **Impact:** Delays in program development
- **Mitigation:** Early start, dedicated resources

**Program Development Risks:**
- **Risk:** Delays in program development
- **Impact:** Delays in program launch
- **Mitigation:** Early start, adequate resources

**Partnership Risks:**
- **Risk:** Partnership delays
- **Impact:** Reduced program support
- **Mitigation:** Early partnership development, multiple partners

**Funding Risks:**
- **Risk:** Funding delays or shortfalls
- **Impact:** Program limitations
- **Mitigation:** Diversified funding, early applications

**Timeline Risks:**
- **Risk:** Timeline delays
- **Impact:** Missed Spring-Summer 2026 deadline
- **Mitigation:** Buffer time, parallel activities
