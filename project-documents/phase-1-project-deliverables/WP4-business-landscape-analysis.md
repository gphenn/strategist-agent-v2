## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Current Business Landscape Overview

Baie Verte has a diverse retail and service business sector with approximately 15+ identified businesses generating over $20M in combined annual revenue. The business community is primarily retail-focused, with some service and manufacturing capabilities. Key opportunities exist for expanding into mining support services to capture 80-85% of Firefly Metals service contracts.

### 1.2 Key Findings

- **Business Concentration**: Retail trade dominates the business landscape
- **Revenue Distribution**: Top businesses generate $0.4M - $5.5M annually
- **Service Gaps**: Limited mining support service businesses identified
- **Opportunity**: Significant potential for new service businesses and expansion of existing businesses
- **Capacity Building Needed**: Existing businesses may need capacity building to meet mining service requirements

### 1.3 Strategic Implications

- **Service Development**: Need to develop new service businesses for mining support
- **Business Expansion**: Existing businesses can expand into related service areas
- **Training & Support**: Business capacity building programs needed
- **Partnership Opportunities**: Potential for partnerships between existing businesses and new service providers
