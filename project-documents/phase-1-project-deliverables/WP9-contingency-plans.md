## Document Purpose

This document provides contingency plans for critical and high-priority risks. Plans will be refined with stakeholder input and technical assessments.

**Objective:** Develop comprehensive contingency plans to respond effectively if risks materialize, minimizing impact and enabling recovery.

---

## Contingency Planning Framework

### Contingency Plan Components

**For Each Risk:**
- **Trigger Points:** Conditions that activate contingency plan
- **Immediate Response:** Actions to take immediately
- **Recovery Actions:** Steps to recover from risk impact
- **Resources Required:** People, budget, time needed
- **Communication Plan:** Who to notify and how
- **Success Criteria:** How to measure recovery success

---

## 1. Critical Risk Contingency Plans

### Risk R001: Services Not Ready by Spring-Summer 2026 Deadline

#### Contingency Plan

**Trigger Points:**
- Infrastructure completion behind schedule by 3+ months
- Workforce development behind schedule by 3+ months
- Service business development behind schedule by 3+ months
- Any critical path activity delayed by 2+ months

**Immediate Response (First 48 Hours):**
1. **Assess Situation:**
   - Evaluate current status
   - Identify specific delays
   - Assess impact on deadline
   - Identify available options

2. **Notify Stakeholders:**
   - Notify Firefly Metals of potential delay
   - Notify steering committee
   - Notify project team
   - Prepare communication plan

3. **Activate Contingency Team:**
   - Assemble contingency response team
   - Assign responsibilities
   - Establish communication channels
   - Begin contingency planning

**Recovery Actions (First Week):**
1. **Develop Recovery Plan:**
   - Identify recovery options
   - Assess feasibility of options
   - Select optimal recovery approach
   - Develop detailed recovery plan

2. **Accelerate Activities:**
   - Identify activities that can be accelerated
   - Allocate additional resources
   - Implement acceleration measures
   - Monitor acceleration progress

3. **Alternative Solutions:**
   - Identify alternative service providers
   - Assess alternative approaches
   - Implement alternatives if feasible
   - Monitor alternative solutions

**Recovery Actions (First Month):**
1. **Reallocate Resources:**
   - Identify available resources
   - Reallocate to critical activities
   - Monitor resource utilization
   - Adjust as needed

2. **Negotiate Timeline Extension:**
   - Assess timeline extension feasibility
   - Negotiate with Firefly Metals
   - Adjust project timeline
   - Update all stakeholders

3. **Implement Parallel Activities:**
   - Identify parallel activity opportunities
   - Implement parallel activities
   - Monitor parallel progress
   - Coordinate activities

**Resources Required:**
- **People:** Additional project team members, contractors, consultants
- **Budget:** Additional funding for acceleration, alternatives
- **Time:** Extended timeline if negotiation successful
- **Equipment:** Additional equipment if needed

**Communication Plan:**
- **Immediate:** Firefly Metals, steering committee, project team
- **Within 24 Hours:** Municipal council, key stakeholders
- **Within 48 Hours:** Public communication if needed
- **Ongoing:** Regular status updates

**Success Criteria:**
- Services operational by deadline or negotiated extension
- Service quality maintained
- Stakeholder relationships maintained
- Project objectives achieved

**Status:** Framework - Requires Detailed Plan Development

## 3. Contingency Plan Management

### Plan Activation Procedures

**Activation Criteria:**
- Risk materializes
- Trigger points reached
- Risk impact exceeds threshold
- Stakeholder decision

**Activation Process:**
1. Identify risk materialization
2. Assess situation
3. Activate appropriate contingency plan
4. Notify stakeholders
5. Begin recovery actions

### Plan Review and Update

**Review Frequency:**
- Monthly review of critical risk plans
- Quarterly review of all plans
- Update after risk events
- Update after significant changes

**Update Process:**
1. Review plan effectiveness
2. Assess plan relevance
3. Update plans as needed
4. Communicate updates
5. Test updated plans
