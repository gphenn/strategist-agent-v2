## Document Purpose

This document provides an implementation timeline for infrastructure development requirements. The timeline will be developed after infrastructure development requirements are defined and detailed planning is completed.

**Objective:** Provide comprehensive implementation timeline to guide infrastructure development execution, coordinate with service development timeline, and ensure readiness for Spring-Summer 2026 service operations.

**Critical Timeline:** Services must be operational when Firefly needs them (Spring-Summer 2026 start)

---

## Timeline Framework

### Timeline Phases

**Phase 1: Critical Infrastructure (Spring-Summer 2026)**
- Timeline: Immediate to Spring-Summer 2026
- Infrastructure: Highway, Roads, Power, Water, Sewer
- Objective: Enable service development and operations

**Phase 2: High Priority Infrastructure (1-2 Years)**
- Timeline: 2026-2028
- Infrastructure: Healthcare
- Objective: Support workforce and community

**Phase 3: Medium Priority Infrastructure (3-5 Years)**
- Timeline: 2026-2030
- Infrastructure: Telecommunications, Municipal Services
- Objective: Long-term sustainability

## Timeline Risk Assessment

### Timeline Risks

**Planning Risks:**
- **Risk:** Delays in planning
- **Impact:** Delays in design and construction
- **Mitigation:** Early planning start, dedicated resources

**Design Risks:**
- **Risk:** Delays in design
- **Impact:** Delays in approvals and construction
- **Mitigation:** Early design start, adequate resources

**Approval Risks:**
- **Risk:** Delays in approvals
- **Impact:** Delays in construction
- **Mitigation:** Early approval applications, proactive engagement

**Construction Risks:**
- **Risk:** Delays in construction
- **Impact:** Missed Spring-Summer 2026 deadline
- **Mitigation:** Buffer time, parallel activities, early start

**Weather Risks:**
- **Risk:** Weather delays
- **Impact:** Construction delays
- **Mitigation:** Weather contingency, indoor work where possible
