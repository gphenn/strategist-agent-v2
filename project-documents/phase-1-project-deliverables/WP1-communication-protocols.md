## Document Purpose

This document establishes comprehensive communication protocols for the Baie Verte Economic Development Project. These protocols ensure consistent, timely, and effective communication across all stakeholders, project team members, and working groups throughout Phase 1 and subsequent phases.

**Objectives:**

- Ensure all stakeholders receive appropriate information at the right time
- Maintain transparency and build trust through consistent communication
- Facilitate efficient decision-making through clear communication channels
- Document all key communications for project records
- Support stakeholder engagement and alignment

---

## Communication Principles

### Core Principles

1. **Transparency:** Open and honest communication with all stakeholders
2. **Timeliness:** Information shared at appropriate times to support decision-making
3. **Clarity:** Clear, concise, and jargon-free communication
4. **Consistency:** Standardized formats and processes across all communications
5. **Accessibility:** Information accessible to all stakeholders regardless of technical background
6. **Accountability:** Clear ownership and responsibility for communications
7. **Confidentiality:** Appropriate handling of sensitive information

### Communication Standards

- **Professional Tone:** All communications maintain professional, respectful tone
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** Respectful of Indigenous communities and cultural protocols
- **Bilingual Consideration:** Accommodate English and French as needed
- **Plain Language:** Avoid technical jargon; explain when necessary
- **Action-Oriented:** Communications include clear next steps and responsibilities

## Communication Types & Protocols

### 1. Internal Communications

#### 1.1 Project Team Communications

**Daily Stand-ups (Optional):**
- **Frequency:** Daily (if needed during critical periods)
- **Duration:** 15 minutes
- **Format:** Brief status, blockers, priorities
- **Owner:** Project Manager
- **Participants:** Core project team
- **Tools:** In-person, video conference, or collaboration platform

**Weekly Team Meetings:**
- **Frequency:** Every Monday (or first working day of week)
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Format:** 
  - Review previous week accomplishments
  - Plan current week priorities
  - Discuss blockers and risks
  - Review budget and timeline
- **Owner:** Project Manager
- **Participants:** Core project team, working group leads
- **Tools:** In-person preferred, video conference acceptable
- **Documentation:** Meeting minutes within 24 hours

**Working Group Meetings:**
- **Frequency:** Weekly (each working group)
- **Duration:** 60-90 minutes
- **Format:** 
  - Review work package progress
  - Discuss technical issues
  - Coordinate with other working groups
- **Owner:** Working Group Lead
- **Participants:** Working group members, subject matter experts
- **Documentation:** Meeting notes shared with Project Manager

#### 1.2 Steering Committee Communications

**Bi-weekly Steering Committee Meetings:**
- **Frequency:** Every two weeks
- **Duration:** 90 minutes
- **Format:**
  - Executive summary of progress
  - Key decisions required
  - Risk and issue review
  - Budget and timeline status
- **Owner:** Project Manager
- **Participants:** Steering Committee members
- **Documentation:** Decision log and meeting minutes within 48 hours

**Decision Briefs:**
- **Frequency:** As needed (minimum 48 hours before decisions required)
- **Format:** 
  - Issue/opportunity description
  - Options analysis
  - Recommendation
  - Impact assessment
  - Decision required
- **Owner:** Project Manager
- **Distribution:** Steering Committee members
- **Response Time:** 48 hours for non-urgent, 24 hours for urgent

#### 1.3 Escalation Communications

**Issue Escalation:**
- **Level 1 (Working Group):** Working group lead addresses within working group
- **Level 2 (Project Manager):** Escalate to Project Manager if unresolved in 48 hours
- **Level 3 (Steering Committee):** Escalate to Steering Committee if unresolved in 1 week
- **Urgent Issues:** Immediate escalation to appropriate level
- **Format:** Issue log with description, impact, proposed resolution, escalation path

**Risk Escalation:**
- **Low Risk:** Documented in risk register, reviewed weekly
- **Medium Risk:** Escalated to Project Manager, mitigation plan required
- **High Risk:** Escalated to Steering Committee immediately, action plan required
- **Format:** Risk register update with escalation notification

- **Format:** Council report format, executive summary
- **Owner:** CAO
- **Distribution:** Council members, public (if required)

- **Format:**

- **Owner:** Project Manager
- **Distribution:** Firefly Metals project contacts
- **Response Protocol:** Acknowledge within 24 hours, respond to questions within 48 hours

- **Response Protocol:** Address questions and concerns, follow-up on commitments

**Neighboring Communities:**
- **Frequency:** Quarterly updates
- **Format:** 
  - Regional coordination updates

- **Owner:** Stakeholder Coordinator
- **Distribution:** Community leaders, regional organizations
- **Response Protocol:** Acknowledge receipt, respond to inquiries

#### 2.4 Media Communications

**Press Releases:**
- **Frequency:** Major milestones, significant announcements
- **Format:** Standard press release format
- **Owner:** Communications Specialist
- **Distribution:** Local and provincial media
- **Approval:** Project Manager and Steering Committee Chair
- **Response Protocol:** Media inquiries handled within 24 hours

**Media Briefings:**
- **Frequency:** As needed for major announcements
- **Format:** Press conference or media briefing
- **Owner:** Communications Specialist
- **Participants:** Project Manager, Steering Committee Chair, key stakeholders
- **Preparation:** Media kit, key messages, Q&A preparation

## Emergency & Crisis Communications

### Emergency Communication Protocol

1. **Immediate Notification:** Project Manager notified immediately
2. **Assessment:** Situation assessed within 1 hour
3. **Stakeholder Notification:** Key stakeholders notified within 2 hours
4. **Public Communication:** Public communication within 4 hours (if required)
5. **Ongoing Updates:** Regular updates until situation resolved

### Crisis Communication Team

- **Project Manager:** Overall coordination
- **Communications Specialist:** Media and public communications
- **Stakeholder Coordinator:** Stakeholder notifications
- **Steering Committee Chair:** Strategic decisions

### Crisis Communication Channels

- **Internal:** Immediate team notification
- **Stakeholders:** Direct notification to key stakeholders
- **Public:** Press release, website update, social media (if applicable)
- **Media:** Media briefing if required
