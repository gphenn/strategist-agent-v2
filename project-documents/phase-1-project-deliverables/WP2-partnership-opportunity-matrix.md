## Document Purpose

This matrix identifies and evaluates partnership opportunities between all three mining/industrial companies, local businesses, and community stakeholders. The matrix will be populated with specific partnership opportunities following direct engagement with each company.

**Objective:** Identify partnership opportunities that support local economic development, service contract capture, and community benefits across all three companies.

**Targets:** Maximize local service contract capture across all operations

## Partnership Evaluation Framework

### Evaluation Criteria

**1. Strategic Alignment**
- Alignment with economic development objectives
- Support for local service capture target (80-85%)
- Community benefit potential
- Long-term sustainability

**2. Business Feasibility**
- Local business capacity
- Financial feasibility
- Technical capability
- Resource availability

**3. Partnership Viability**
- Partner compatibility
- Risk sharing
- Mutual benefit
- Long-term commitment

**4. Implementation Readiness**
- Timeline alignment (Spring-Summer 2026)
- Capacity building requirements
- Resource requirements
- Regulatory compliance

## Partnership Success Factors

### Critical Success Factors

1. **Clear Partnership Objectives:** Well-defined goals and expectations
2. **Mutual Benefit:** Benefits for all partners
3. **Strong Relationships:** Trust and communication
4. **Capacity Building:** Support for local business development
5. **Performance Management:** Clear metrics and monitoring
6. **Flexibility:** Ability to adapt and adjust

### Partnership Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Capacity Gaps**
- **Mitigation:** Capacity building programs, phased implementation

**Risk 2: Partnership Conflicts**
- **Mitigation:** Clear agreements, regular communication, conflict resolution processes

**Risk 3: Timeline Pressure**
- **Mitigation:** Early partnership development, capacity building support

**Risk 4: Financial Constraints**
- **Mitigation:** Funding support, financial planning, access to capital
