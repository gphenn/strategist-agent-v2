## Document Purpose

This matrix provides a comprehensive analysis of service provider opportunities for capturing 80-85% of Firefly Metals mining service contracts locally. The matrix identifies current business capacity, service gaps, opportunities, and required actions for each service category.

**Objective:** Enable systematic identification and development of local service providers to meet Firefly Metals service requirements.

**Target:** 80-85% of mining service contracts captured locally (from strategic context brief)
