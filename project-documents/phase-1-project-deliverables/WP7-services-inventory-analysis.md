## Document Purpose

This document provides a framework for inventorying and analyzing community services in Baie Verte. The inventory will be completed with current services data collection.

**Objective:** Provide comprehensive inventory of community services to assess current capacity and identify service gaps.

**Target:** Support population growth from 1,315 to 2,311-2,511 (projected) and workforce expansion

## Regional Service Centre Analysis

### Baie Verte as Regional Service Centre

**Catchment Area:**
- **Total Population:** 2,797 (2016) - communities seeking services in Baie Verte
- **Communities:** At least 10 communities on roads that must pass through Baie Verte
- **Service Area:** Top of Baie Verte Peninsula

**Service Demand:**
- Baie Verte serves as regional service centre
- Communities seek services in Baie Verte before travelling to Springdale or beyond
- Additional service demand from catchment area

**Service Capacity Implications:**
- Services must serve both Baie Verte and catchment area
- Service capacity planning must account for regional demand
- Service expansion may benefit regional communities

**Status:** ⚠️ Requires regional service demand assessment
