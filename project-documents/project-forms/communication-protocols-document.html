4.2 Decision Briefs

üìù Decision Brief Format

Decision briefs are prepared for Steering Committee decisions and distributed minimum 48 hours before decisions are required.

Issue/Opportunity Clear description of the matter requiring decision
Options Analysis Pros, cons, and implications of each option
Recommendation Clear recommendation with rationale
Impact Assessment Budget, timeline, resource, and risk implications
Decision Required Specific decision or approval being requested

Response Time: 48 hours for non-urgent matters, 24 hours for urgent matters
