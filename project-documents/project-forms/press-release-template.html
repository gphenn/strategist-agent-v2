About the Baie Verte Economic Development Project

The Baie Verte Economic Development Project is a multi-company initiative bringing together Firefly Metals, New Found Gold, and Shoreline Aggregates to support sustainable economic growth in the Baie Verte region of Newfoundland and Labrador. The project focuses on infrastructure development, workforce training, housing solutions, and business opportunities that will benefit the community for generations to come.
