# Implementation Roadmap: Baie Verte Mining Expansion Economic Development

## Strategic Specialist (Morgan) - Detailed Action Planning

## Executive Summary

This implementation roadmap provides detailed action plans, timelines, responsibilities, and resource requirements for executing the Baie Verte mining expansion economic development strategy.

## Implementation Overview

### Timeline Summary

- **Phase 1 (Foundation)**: Q1-Q2 2025 - Stakeholder alignment and coordination
- **Phase 2 (Development)**: Q3-Q4 2025 - Capacity building and preparation
- **Phase 3 (Launch)**: Q1-Q2 2026 - Service delivery initiation
- **Phase 4 (Optimization)**: Q3-Q4 2026 - Performance optimization

### Resource Requirements Summary

- **Total Estimated Investment**: $15-20 million over 2 years
- **Human Resources**: 8-12 dedicated staff positions
- **Infrastructure Investment**: $8-12 million
- **Business Development Support**: $3-5 million
- **Workforce Development**: $2-3 million
