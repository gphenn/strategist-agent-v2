# Performance Monitoring & Optimization

**Strategic Specialist (Morgan) - Performance Management & Continuous Improvement**

## Executive Summary

This document provides the comprehensive performance monitoring and optimization framework for the Baie Verte mining expansion economic development initiative, focusing on systematic performance tracking, evaluation, and continuous improvement.

## Performance Monitoring Framework

### Primary Performance Metrics

#### 1. Service Capture Metrics

**Target**: 80-85% of mining service contracts awarded locally

**Measurement Framework**:

- **Contract Tracking**: Monthly tracking of all mining service contracts
- **Local Award Percentage**: Percentage of contracts awarded to local businesses
- **Contract Value**: Total value of contracts awarded to local businesses
- **Service Categories**: Breakdown by service category (food, transportation, etc.)

**Data Collection**:

- **Source**: Firefly Metals procurement data, local business reporting
- **Frequency**: Monthly collection and reporting
- **Responsibility**: Performance Management Coordinator
- **Tools**: Contract tracking database, reporting dashboard

**Success Criteria**:

- **Q1 2026**: 60-70% of early-phase contracts captured
- **Q2 2026**: 70-80% of operational contracts captured
- **Q3 2026**: 80-85% of all contracts captured
- **Q4 2026**: 80-85% maintained with quality standards

#### 2. Employment Impact Metrics

**Target**: 1.5-2x employment multiplier beyond direct mining employment

**Measurement Framework**:

- **Direct Employment**: Direct employment in mining services
- **Indirect Employment**: Indirect employment in supporting industries
- **Induced Employment**: Induced employment from economic spending
- **Employment Multiplier**: Total employment divided by direct mining employment

**Data Collection**:

- **Source**: Local business employment data, economic impact analysis
- **Frequency**: Monthly collection, quarterly analysis
- **Responsibility**: Performance Management Coordinator
- **Tools**: Employment tracking system, economic impact modeling

**Success Criteria**:

- **Q1 2026**: 1.2-1.5x employment multiplier achieved
- **Q2 2026**: 1.5-1.8x employment multiplier achieved
- **Q3 2026**: 1.8-2.0x employment multiplier achieved
- **Q4 2026**: 1.5-2.0x employment multiplier maintained

#### 3. Infrastructure Readiness Metrics

**Target**: All critical infrastructure completed by Q2 2026

**Measurement Framework**:

- **Project Completion**: Percentage completion of infrastructure projects
- **Timeline Adherence**: On-time completion of infrastructure projects
- **Budget Management**: Within-budget completion of infrastructure projects
- **Quality Standards**: Meeting or exceeding quality standards

**Data Collection**:

- **Source**: Infrastructure project management data, contractor reports
- **Frequency**: Weekly collection, monthly reporting
- **Responsibility**: Infrastructure Project Manager
- **Tools**: Project management system, progress tracking dashboard

**Success Criteria**:

- **Q1 2025**: Infrastructure planning completed
- **Q2 2025**: Infrastructure funding secured
- **Q3 2025**: Infrastructure construction initiated
- **Q4 2025**: 50% of infrastructure projects completed
- **Q1 2026**: 75% of infrastructure projects completed
- **Q2 2026**: 100% of infrastructure projects completed

#### 4. Funding Secured Metrics

**Target**: All required funding committed from diversified sources

**Measurement Framework**:

- **Funding Commitments**: Total funding committed from all sources
- **Funding Disbursements**: Total funding disbursed and utilized
- **Funding Sources**: Breakdown by funding source (provincial, federal, municipal, private)
- **Funding Timeline**: Adherence to funding disbursement schedules

**Data Collection**:

- **Source**: Funding agency data, financial management system
- **Frequency**: Monthly collection and reporting
- **Responsibility**: Project Director
- **Tools**: Financial tracking system, funding dashboard

**Success Criteria**:

- **Q1 2025**: 25% of required funding committed
- **Q2 2025**: 50% of required funding committed
- **Q3 2025**: 75% of required funding committed
- **Q4 2025**: 100% of required funding committed
- **Q1 2026**: 25% of funding disbursed and utilized
- **Q2 2026**: 50% of funding disbursed and utilized

#### 5. Stakeholder Alignment Metrics

**Target**: Formal coordination mechanisms established and operational

**Measurement Framework**:

- **Coordination Mechanisms**: Number and effectiveness of coordination mechanisms
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Level and quality of stakeholder engagement
- **Communication Effectiveness**: Effectiveness of communication and information sharing
- **Decision-Making Efficiency**: Efficiency and effectiveness of decision-making processes

**Data Collection**:

- **Source**: Stakeholder feedback, meeting evaluations, satisfaction surveys
- **Frequency**: Monthly collection, quarterly analysis
- **Responsibility**: Municipal Economic Development Officer
- **Tools**: Stakeholder satisfaction surveys, meeting evaluation forms

**Success Criteria**:

- **Q1 2025**: Coordination committee established
- **Q2 2025**: Stakeholder engagement protocols implemented
- **Q3 2025**: Regular coordination meetings operational
- **Q4 2025**: Stakeholder satisfaction at 80% or higher
- **Q1 2026**: Stakeholder satisfaction at 85% or higher
- **Q2 2026**: Stakeholder satisfaction at 90% or higher
